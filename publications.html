Selected Publications

Highlighted works related to human-centric knowledge systems, multimodal digital twins and simulation-driven action understanding.

MetalMind: A Knowledge Graph-Driven Human-Centric Knowledge System for Metal Additive Manufacturing
Haolin Fan, Zhen Fan, Chenshu Liu, Jianhao Zhu, Tom Gibbs, Jerry Ying Hsi Fuh, Wen Feng Lu, Bingbing Li
npj Advanced Manufacturing, 2025.
Journal article · Human-centric knowledge systems · Knowledge graph + RAG + MR
Proposes a human-centric knowledge system that combines LLM-powered knowledge graph construction, hybrid (vector + graph) retrieval, and mixed reality interfaces to support training and decision-making in metal additive manufacturing.
DOI
Sim2Know: New Paradigm of Digital Twins to Design and Inform Human-Centric Knowledge System
Bingbing Li, Haolin Fan, Zhen Fan, John Ahmet Erkoyuncu, Hong-Chao Zhang
CIRP Annals – Manufacturing Technology, 2025.
CIRP Annals · Digital twin · Sim-to-real action recognition
Introduces the Sim2Know framework that uses digital twins and synthetic data to train action recognition models and connects them to a multimodal knowledge graph, enabling human-centric guidance and implicit knowledge capture in metal AM workflows.
