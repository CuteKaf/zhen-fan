Research Vision

My research aims to bridge the gap between Simulation and Reality. I explore the "Next Step" in Embodied AI: moving from pure scaling laws to Test-Time Compute (System 2 Thinking) and Explainable Collaboration.

Sim-to-Real Transfer & Digital Twins

Omniverse Synthetic Data

Collecting labeled real-world data is expensive. I leverage high-fidelity simulation (Isaac Sim) to build digital twins. By simulating physical properties and interactions, I generate large-scale synthetic data to train robot perception models, effectively bridging the reality gap.

Neuro-Symbolic Alignment

VLA Models Reasoning

End-to-end VLA models often act as "Black Boxes." My research focuses on designing a Neuro-Symbolic Bridge where the "probabilistic" intents generated by Large Models are verified against "deterministic" symbolic constraints (Knowledge Graphs) to ensure safety.

Cognitive Manufacturing Agents

Knowledge Graphs HRC

Developing intelligent agents powered by Domain Knowledge Graphs and LLMs. These agents can digest unstructured technical manuals and provide expert-level decision support, creating a loop where humans teach robots, and robots explain their reasoning.
