Research Vision

My research is motivated by the rapid advancement of collaborative robots (Cobots) in manufacturing. Despite progress, current HRC systems often lack the intuitive communication and reasoning capabilities required for true human-like collaboration.

The Core Question

How can we enable seamless communication and leverage human cognitive abilities in Human-Robot Collaboration?

To address this, I propose a new paradigm that mirrors human-human collaboration:

  • Perception (The Eyes): Using High-Fidelity Digital Twins to generate synthetic data, solving the data scarcity problem in industrial environments.
  • Reasoning (The Cerebrum): Employing Neuro-Symbolic AI (VLA Models + Knowledge Graphs) to enable robots to understand high-level intent while adhering to strict safety constraints.
  • Interaction (The Bridge): Creating Bi-directional Interfaces (via Mixed Reality or Language) that allow experts to teach robots and robots to explain their actions.

My ultimate goal is to move Embodied AI from pure "scaling laws" to Test-Time Compute (System 2 Thinking), ensuring robot behaviors are not only intelligent but also verifiable and safe.
