About Me

I am a Master's student at NUS and a Digitalization Engineer, passionate about bridging the gap between simulation and reality in robotics.

My research focuses on **Digital Twins, Neuro-Symbolic AI, and Human-Robot Collaboration**. I aim to build systems where robots not only "act" based on probabilities but also "reason" based on structured knowledge and physical constraints.

Technically, I specialize in **NVIDIA Omniverse (Isaac Sim)** for high-fidelity synthetic data generation. My recent work focuses on building **Cognitive Agents** by integrating **Named Entity Recognition (NER)**, **Knowledge Graphs (KG)**, and **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** with Multimodal Large Models (MMLM) to parse complex industrial knowledge.
