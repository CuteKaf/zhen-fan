About Me

I am a Master's student at NUS and a Digitalization Engineer, passionate about bridging the gap between simulation and reality in robotics[cite: 74, 88].

My research focuses on Digital Twins, Neuro-Symbolic AI, and Human-Robot Collaboration. I aim to build systems where robots not only "act" based on probabilities but also "reason" based on structured knowledge (Knowledge Graphs) and physical constraints[cite: 146, 160].

I have experience with NVIDIA Omniverse (Isaac Sim) for synthetic data generation and building Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for manufacturing scenarios[cite: 101].
